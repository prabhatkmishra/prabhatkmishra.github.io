    Prabhat Kumar Mishra

    I am an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IITKgp), India, and currently working at the intersection of control and learning. I am developing algorithms for autonomous systems to make them smarter with the help of data and distribution. I use control theory to provide safety certificates.

    I did PhD from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB), India, on Stochastic Model Predictive Control (MPC) for Cyber Physical Systems. During my PhD, I received a Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship to study at the Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne (EPFL), Switzerland, for one year. I learnt Gaussian process regression and implemented stochastic MPC on a quadcopter during my stay at EPFL. Before joining IITKgp, I was a Postdoctoral Associate at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), USA, and working on polynomial chaos theory for biomedical applications. Prior to that, I was a Postdoctoral Research Asssociate at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign (UIUC), USA, where we developed a couple of deep learning supported MPC algorithms and a constrained estimator.
