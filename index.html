About me

I am an Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpyr (IITKgp), India. Before joining IITKgp, I was a Posdoctoral Associate at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), USA. My research work is focused on autonomous systems and machine learning.

I like analysis, probability and optimization. My doctoral work at IIT Bombay, India, presents a provably stabilizing framework for stochastic MPC in the presence of unreliable channels.

I am interested in understanding the theoretical foundation of machine learning, and its applications for safety critical systems and cyber physical system.
